xMatic Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 11, 2024

Welcome to xMatic ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome extension ("Extension").

1. Information We Don't Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect when you use xMatic:

  • We do not collect or store your Twitter/X login credentials
  • We do not collect or store your tweets, direct messages, or any other Twitter/X content
  • We do not track your browsing history or activity outside of our extension
  • We do not collect personal information like your name, email, or location

2. How We Use Your OpenAI API Key

When you provide your OpenAI API key:

  • Your API key is stored locally in your browser's extension storage
  • The key is only used to process your requests through the OpenAI API
  • We do not transmit your API key to any servers we control
  • The key is only sent directly to OpenAI's servers when generating responses

3. Data Processing

When you use xMatic to generate replies:

  • The tweet you're replying to is sent to OpenAI's API to generate a response
  • This data is processed according to OpenAI's privacy policy
  • We do not store any of this data on our servers
  • Your custom style preferences are stored locally in your browser

4. Third-Party Services

xMatic interacts with the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: For generating AI responses
  • Twitter/X: To access and post content (through official APIs)

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information:

  • Your OpenAI API key is stored securely in your browser's extension storage
  • All communication with OpenAI's API is encrypted using HTTPS
  • We follow industry best practices for data security

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

By using xMatic, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
